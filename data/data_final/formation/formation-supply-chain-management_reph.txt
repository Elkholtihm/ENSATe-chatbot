This document presents the curriculum for the Supply Chain Management engineering cycle, coordinated by Hamid SEGHIOUER (hamid.seghiouer@gmail.com). In the first semester students study Languages and TEC 1, Management 1, probability, statistics and stochastic calculus, computer networks 1, relational database fundamentals, and operations research with graph theory. The second semester covers functional analysis and value analysis, production management, modelling tools and performance evaluation, quality management, combinatorial optimisation, and purchasing techniques for cost reduction. The fifth semester includes distribution logistics, information systems in SCM, industrial system simulation, port, rail and airport logistics, Management 3, and Languages and TEC 3. The final semester culminates in a capstone project.
