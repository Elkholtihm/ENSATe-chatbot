This document outlines the curriculum for a two‑year preparatory program. The coordinator is Amel NEJJARI, reachable at amelnejjari@yahoo.fr. In the first semester, students study Algebra I, Analysis I, Physics I, Mechanics I, Computer Science I, and Language and Communication I. The second semester features Algebra II, Analysis II, Physics II, Chemistry, Computer‑Assisted Mathematics (MAO), and Language and Communication II. In the third semester, the courses include Algebra III, Analysis III, Physics III, Mechanics II, Computer Science II, and Language and Communication III. The fourth semester contains Analysis IV, Applied Mathematics, Physics IV, Electronics, Management, and Language and Communication IV.
