This document details research projects conducted at ENSA Tetouan. In 2020, several thematic projects received funding from Université Abdelmalek Essaadi. One project, led by Professor EL BIARI Aouatif, focuses on early detection of mental disorders in young people using new technologies and has a budget of 90,000 DHS. Another, headed by Professor RAISSOUNI Naoufal, studies the dynamics of terrestrial coverage in the TTA region amid climate warming, employing Earth observation satellites and intensive computing, with a budget of 100,000 DHS. A third project, managed by Professor TAHIRI Abderrahim, proposes an intelligent model for forest fire protection called the Intelligent Tree System, also supported with 100,000 DHS. The Covid‑19 support program in June 2020 introduced a project on automatically controlling exemption travel certificates and monitoring the spread of Covid‑19 in Morocco, overseen by Professor EL YOUNOUSSI Yacine and financed with 75,000 DHS. The university’s list of thematic projects includes a study on smart cities in the Tanger‑Tétouan‑Al Hoceima region, led by Professor ABTOY Anouar, with a budget of 57,000 DHS. Additionally, a PPR2 research project funded by CNRST in 2015 aimed to acquire new scientific or technical knowledge, titled detection of damage in composites using multi‑element CND methods, was directed by Professor KHAMLICHI Abdellatif and allocated 955,000 DHS.
