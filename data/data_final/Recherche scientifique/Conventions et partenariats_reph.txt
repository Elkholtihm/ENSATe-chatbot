This document details the conventions and partnerships of ENSA Tétouan. By acknowledging the value of deepening collaborative relationships, the school has forged agreements with a range of national and international universities, schools, and companies, including Boss Management, Cype Campus, Everis, and others. Among the academic collaborations, there is a partnership with HEG‑SO, whose site can be found at https://www.hesge.ch/heg/, and another with the National School of Architecture in Tétouan, available at http://www.archi.ac.ma/. In France, ENSA Tétouan is currently engaged in an academic exchange with ESIX Normandie at Université Caen Normandie, as well as a joint diploma program with Polytech Angers; further information is posted on https://www.unicaen.fr/structure/esix-normandie/ and http://www.polytech-angers.fr/fr/index.html. Commercial agreements have also been established with SATEC (https://www.satecgroup.com/), Cype Campus (http://www.cype.ma/), Mega Cours, Boss Management, Everis (https://www.everis.com/morocco/fr/home-spain-morocco), DevinWeb (https://devinweb.com/), and Groupe Avalon (https://grupoavalon.es/).
