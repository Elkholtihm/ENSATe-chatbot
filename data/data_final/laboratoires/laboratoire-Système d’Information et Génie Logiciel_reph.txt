The following document pertains to the laboratory known as Système d’Information et Génie Logiciel. This research unit, abbreviated SIGL, operates within the field of computer science and focuses on Information and Communication Systems. Its areas of inquiry include information systems, e‑learning, big data, intelligent systems, software engineering, modeling, and natural language processing.
